To Add a New User:

Go to the Add User tab and fill out the fields and then click Submit.

To Edit a User:

Go to the View Users tab, click the Edit Users icon and change your desired fields, then click Update.

To Delete a User:

Go to the View Users tab, click the Edit Users icon , then click Delete User.

To Create a Report:

Go the the Reports tab, fill out the fields accordingly, then click Generate Report.

To Print a Report:

Go to the Reports tab, fill out the fields accordingly, click Generate Report, and then click Print at the top of the page.

To Export a Report to Excel

Go to the Reports tab, fill out the fields accordingly, click Generate Report, and then click Export at the top of the page.
